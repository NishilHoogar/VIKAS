about us

VIKAS is an online e-commerce platform which aims at dismantling all communication gaps between SHGs and consumers while also strengthening existing trading procedures and laying the establishment of a new era of globalisation for SHGs wherein they can be represented on a massive scale hence enabling them to gain enormous exposure and recognition.

VIKAS also aims at providing the best user experience by providing state of the art user experience and interaction and providing numerous facilities to ease the selection process. The next step to enhance the livelihood and upliftment of SHGs is marketing the products made by SHG members.
